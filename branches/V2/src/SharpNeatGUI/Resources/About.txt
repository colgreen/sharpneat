NEAT is Neuro-Evolution of Augmenting Topologies - Evolution of Neural Networks.

SharpNEAT is an implementation of NEAT created by Colin Green. SharpNEAT is written in C# and targetting the .Net framework. For more information visit http://sharpneat.sourceforge.net/

The NEAT method was devised by Kenneth Stanley, currently of the University of Central Florida School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. Visit http://www.cs.ucf.edu/~kstanley/ for more info on NEAT including research papers, software, source code, a FAQ and an active discussion group.
